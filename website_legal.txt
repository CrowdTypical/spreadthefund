                              PRIVACY POLICY
================================================================================

Spread the Funds collects only the data necessary to provide its core
bill-splitting functionality:

- Google account info (name, email, photo) for authentication
- Group and bill data you create within the app
- Feedback you voluntarily submit

Your data is stored securely in Firebase and is never sold or shared with
third parties.
