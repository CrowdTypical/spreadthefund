# Privacy Policy for Spread the Funds

**Last updated:** March 26, 2026

Jason Green ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Spread the Funds mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Your **email address**
- Your **display name**
- Your **Google profile photo URL**
- A **username** you choose during onboarding

### Financial Data
When you use the App to split bills, we collect:
- **Bill details**: amount, description, category, notes, date, who paid, and split percentages
- **Settlement records**: who paid whom, amounts, and dates

### Group Data
When you create or join groups, we collect:
- **Group names** and settings (icon, color)
- **Member email addresses**
- **Group images** you optionally upload (stored as compressed images)

### Invitations
When you invite someone to a group, we collect:
- The **invitee's email address**
- The **inviter's name** and the **group name**

### Feedback
If you submit feedback through the App, we collect:
- Your **email address**
- The **feedback text** you provide
- The **app version** you are using

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- **Provide the App's functionality**: create accounts, manage groups, track shared bills, and calculate balances
- **Send invitation emails** to people you invite to your groups
- **Improve the App** based on feedback you submit
- **Check for app updates** (we query GitHub for the latest release version — no personal data is sent)

## 3. How We Store Your Information

All data is stored in **Google Firebase Firestore**, hosted by Google Cloud. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. We do not store data on our own servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Their Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Sign-In** | Authentication | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| **Firebase Authentication** | Identity management | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| **Firebase Firestore** | Data storage | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| **Gmail SMTP** | Sending invitation emails | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

We do **not** use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any tracking services.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- With **Google/Firebase** as our infrastructure provider (see above)
- With **other group members** who can see your email, name, and shared bill data within your groups
- When **required by law** or to protect our legal rights

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you have an account. You can delete your data at any time (see below).

## 7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can **delete all your data** directly from the App using the account deletion feature. This permanently removes:
- Your user profile
- Your membership from all groups (groups are deleted if you are the only member)
- All invitations you sent or received
- All feedback you submitted

After deletion, your data cannot be recovered.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), Firebase security rules, and Google's infrastructure security. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
